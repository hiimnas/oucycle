Safety Tips

by John Appleseed

21/04/2024 - 21/05/2025

As cycling is becoming more popular and the spring season comes, it is important to stay safe on the roads. Always wear a helmet, stay visible with bright clothing, and follow traffic laws. Remember to check your bike before each ride to ensure everything is in working order, including brakes, tires, and lights. Especially if using it to commute in everyday life. We have had some close calls recently, especially in the rainy weather of 18/04 and today (21/04) - wet conditions can make roads slippery and reduce visibility for both cyclists and drivers. It is crucial to adjust your riding style in such weather: slow down, increase your braking distance, and be extra cautious at intersections. Our club can provide discounts to gear, as we get it supplied directly from the warehouses in large quantities. If you need anything, please contact us for further information.
